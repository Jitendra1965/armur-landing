What you can record

Keep the full story together.

Firearm records

Structured, searchable, and easy to maintain.

Provenance & documents

Export & ownership

Your data, always accessible.

A record is only truly yours if you can keep it, back it up, and move it when needed.

📤

No lock-in.

Armur is designed around the idea that your records should remain under your control. Export and portability options are central to that philosophy.

The goal is simple: your documentation should remain useful whether you use Armur for a week or for decades.

Why this matters

  • Software changes
  • Devices fail
  • Companies come and go
  • Your records should endure anyway
