Social

A more trustworthy social layer for collectors.

Armur Social can support clubs, collector circles, event participation, and discussion without turning the core product into a noisy feed. The idea is controlled connection, not engagement bait.

Collector circles

Create trusted spaces for people with similar interests, disciplines, or collecting focus.

Smaller, higher-signal groups

Club and association ties

Help users discover and connect with recognized collector associations, clubs, and institutions.

Built on relevance

Event-aware participation

Link range days, collector shows, or exhibitions to people and communities that care about them.

Community with context

What makes this different

Unlike generic social platforms, this layer would be grounded in serious collecting: provenance, shared interests, institutions, expertise, and real-world participation.

  • Private or invite-based community structures
  • Collector and institution credibility signals
  • Conversation tied to objects, events, and expertise
  • Designed to support the vault, not overwhelm it

Why it fits Armur

The social layer makes sense only after trust is established. That is why it belongs under “Explore More in Armur” rather than competing with the vault message on the homepage.
