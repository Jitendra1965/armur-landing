ARMUR Privacy Policy

By using Armur, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Armur requires an account and uses secure cloud storage to operate the service.

1.1 Account Information (Required)

To use Armur, you must create an account. We collect:

  • Email address
  • Encrypted password hash
  • Display name
  • Account identifiers
  • Subscription and billing status (if applicable)

This information is required to authenticate users and provide access to the App.

1.2 Device Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device to improve performance and enable offline functionality, including:

  • Cached firearm records and inventory details
  • Notes and descriptions
  • Photos and media cached for faster access
  • Offline preferences and settings
  • Temporary offline data

If this data is deleted or the App is removed, Hoplontec may not be able to recover it.

1.3 Cloud Storage

Armur stores user data on secure servers to provide the service, including:

  • Firearm entries and metadata
  • Photos and attachments
  • Notes and descriptions
  • Posts, comments, likes, and social interactions
  • Marketplace listings
  • Notifications and preferences
  • App configuration and settings
  • Maintenance and diagnostic logs

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • App crash logs
  • Performance and usage metrics

This information is used solely for:

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Reliability and performance improvements

1.5 User-Generated Content and Marketplace Listings

Armur allows users to create and share content, including marketplace listings, posts, comments, profile information, and images (“User Content”).

Public Visibility. Marketplace listings may be visible to other users of the App. Profile display names, listing descriptions, and uploaded images may be viewable by other users. Any information you voluntarily include in listings or posts may be seen by others.

Responsibility for Content

You are responsible for the information you choose to share. We recommend that users do not include sensitive personal information (such as home addresses, serial numbers, or government IDs) in public listings or posts.

Storage of User Content

Hoplontec stores User Content to provide:

  • Marketplace functionality
  • Community/social features
  • Content moderation and safety

Content Moderation

We may review, remove, or restrict content that:

  • Violates our Terms of Service
  • Violates applicable laws
  • Creates safety or security risks
  • Is reported by users

2. How We Use Your Information

Hoplontec uses collected information to:

  • Synchronize your collection across devices
  • Provide cloud backup and restoration
  • Enable social and community features
  • Provide marketplace and user-generated content features
  • Moderate and enforce community standards
  • Deliver notifications
  • Maintain and improve the App
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Process subscriptions and manage billing through our payment provider

We do not sell personal data.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help operate the App, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Authentication providers
  • Payment processors (Stripe)
  • Analytics providers (non-personal data only)

Content may be visible to other users as part of marketplace and social features. We do not share firearm data, photos, or personal content with advertisers or marketers without your explicit consent.

We may disclose information:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To respond to lawful government requests
  • To protect the rights and safety of Hoplontec, users, or others

3.1 Payments

If you purchase a subscription or paid features within the App, payments are processed by Stripe, Inc.

  • Hoplontec does not collect, store, or process credit or debit card numbers.
  • Payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe.
  • We may receive limited information such as:
    • Payment status
    • Subscription status
    • Customer ID and billing email
    • Country or region for tax compliance

Stripe’s Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

4. Data Security

Hoplontec implements industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Password hashing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Backup redundancy

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

Delete Your Account

You may request account deletion at any time. All cloud-stored data will be permanently deleted, subject to backup retention.

Export Your Data

We provide tools to export your collection where supported.

6. Data Retention

  • Data is retained until account deletion.
  • Deleted data may temporarily persist in encrypted backups before permanent removal.

7. Children’s Privacy

Armur is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

Hoplontec may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of material changes.
