Quick facts

Your vault is private by default. You choose what (if anything) to share publicly. An account is required to secure access and enable optional sync.

Account required Cloud sync (encrypted) Local-only mode Selective public gallery

Armur is a secure digital vault designed for firearm collectors. It helps you document your collection, preserve provenance, track maintenance and range sessions, and optionally share selected pieces publicly — while keeping you in control of your data.

It is not social media. It is not a marketplace operator. It is a structured companion to the modern gunroom.

You create an account, choose your storage mode (cloud sync or local-only), and begin adding firearms to your vault. For each firearm, you can record:

  • Manufacturer, model, and configuration
  • Photos and attachments
  • Provenance documents and invoices
  • Maintenance and service history
  • Round counts
  • Range sessions and performance notes

Over time, your vault becomes a structured, living record of your collection.

Yes. An account is required to access Armur.

Your account secures your vault and enables authentication, optional encrypted cloud sync, and device access management. Even in local-only mode, an account is required to protect and structure your records.

Cloud Sync Mode
Your encrypted data is securely stored and synced across devices. This provides backup and access from multiple devices.

Local Mode
Your data remains stored only on your device. Nothing is synced to the cloud.

You may switch modes later through guided migration in Settings.

Armur is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No public profiles by default
  • No third-party ad networks
  • No selling of user data
  • Clear separation between private vault and optional public gallery
  • Encrypted cloud infrastructure (when Cloud Sync is enabled)

Armur is built to be discreet and uninteresting to anyone but you.
